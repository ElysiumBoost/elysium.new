Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data We Collect

We collect only the information needed to run your account and deliver your orders. That includes your account email and username, an optional Discord handle if you choose to link one, the configuration and history of every order you place, and the avatar you select for your profile. When you check out, we record the details of the service you purchased so it can be tracked and supported. We do not collect more than is necessary, and we avoid storing sensitive game credentials wherever a token-based login is available. You stay in control of optional fields and can leave them blank without losing access to the core service.

How We Use It

Your information is used to deliver and track the orders you place, to provide support through your Discord ticket, and to manage your account and preferences across sessions. Order history powers your dashboard so you can see status, past purchases, and progress at a glance. Your email lets us send confirmations and important service notices, and your selected avatar and username personalize your profile. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or resale. Processing is limited to operating the service you asked for and keeping it secure, reliable, and easy to use whenever you return.

Supabase Storage

Account and order data is stored using Supabase, which runs on a managed PostgreSQL database. We rely on row-level security policies so that each user can read and modify only their own rows, never anyone else's. This means your orders, profile, and preferences are isolated at the database layer rather than only in the interface. Access is authenticated, and queries are scoped to your authenticated identity. We follow least-privilege principles for any server-side access and keep configuration and keys out of the public site. This approach lets us offer a responsive dashboard while keeping each account's data cleanly separated and protected.

Third Parties

We share data with a deliberately small set of providers, limited to what keeps the service running. Our payment processor handles transactions through its own secure systems, and Supabase provides the database and infrastructure described above. We do not sell your data, and we do not embed advertising trackers or sell profiles to marketers. Any provider we use is bound to handle data only for the purpose of supporting Elysium Boost. We avoid unnecessary integrations, and we review the tools we depend on so that your information stays within a trusted, minimal chain rather than scattered across third-party networks.

Your Rights

You have meaningful control over your information. You can access the data tied to your account, request an export of it in a portable form, and ask us to delete your account and associated records. Because data is scoped per user, removing your account clears the rows that belong to you. You can update your email, username, Discord handle, and avatar at any time from your account. If you want to exercise any of these rights, reach out and we will verify your identity before acting on the request to keep your account protected against impersonation.

Contact

For any privacy question or request, including access, export, or deletion, contact us through our Discord. Our team monitors tickets and will respond as promptly as we can, verifying ownership of the account before making changes. Discord is the fastest way to reach a real person on the team, and it keeps your request in one place alongside any follow-up. If your inquiry concerns a specific order, including the order ID helps us resolve it quickly and confirm that the request is coming from the account holder.

This page is a plain-language summary of our privacy practices and is not a substitute for formal legal advice. For specific questions about your rights, please consult a qualified professional.
