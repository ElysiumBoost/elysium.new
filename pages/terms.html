Service Description

Elysium Boost provides manual, human-performed game boosting and coaching services across the titles listed on our site. Every order is completed by a verified player on real, live matches. We do not use cheats, scripts, bots, macros, or any form of automation, and we never tamper with game files or memory. Coaching sessions are delivered live by ranked specialists who review your gameplay and teach repeatable improvements. Our goal is a clean, legitimate result that holds up over time. Because outcomes depend on live matchmaking, completion windows are estimates rather than guarantees, though we work continuously to beat the times we quote you.

Order Process

Orders are configured directly on the site: you select the game, service, and any add-ons, and the live total updates as you choose. Checkout is handled through our secure on-site flow, and once payment clears your order is confirmed instantly with a unique order ID. That ID lets you track progress inside your account dashboard, where status updates appear as each milestone is reached. You can reach support at any stage through your linked Discord ticket, and our team keeps you informed of meaningful changes such as start time, assigned specialist, and estimated completion. We recommend keeping your contact details current so confirmations and updates always arrive without delay.

Account Safety
